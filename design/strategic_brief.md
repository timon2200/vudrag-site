# Strategic Design Brief: The Digital Monograph

> **Philosophy:** "Timeless elegance with a modern edge."
> **Role:** A digital brand embassy, not an online store.
> **Key Emotion:** Quiet, confident, weighted, exclusive.

## ðŸ›ï¸ Website Structure & Narrative

The site is structured as a journey, not a catalog.

### 1. The Atelier / Artist (Homepage)
*   **Content:** Full-screen video loop (working hands, clay, marble dust).
*   **Text:** Minimal. Name + Philosophy quote ("Form follows emotion").
*   **Goal:** Establish the "Brand Universe."

### 2. The Collection (Not "Products")
*   **Organization:** Grouped by **Series** or **Narrative Themes** (e.g., "Elemental Fragments").
*   **Format:** Dedicated micro-sites for each series.

### 3. A Singular Sculpture Page (The Core)
*   **Hierarchy:**
    1.  **Hero:** Full-viewport, monochrome image. No text.
    2.  **Essentials:** Title, Year, Material, Edition (e.g., "1/8"). Elegant typography.
    3.  **The Story:** Poetic curator's note.
    4.  **Artistry in Detail:** Interactive grid of macro photography.
    5.  **Provenance:** Exhibition history.
    6.  **Acquire:** "Request the Portfolio" / "Inquire for Availability". **No public prices.**

### 4. Commissioning Journey
*   **Content:** Explanation of the bespoke process (consultation -> maquette -> unique legacy piece).
*   **Goal:** Sell the ultimate luxuryâ€”collaboration.

### 5. The Archive (Client Portal)
*   **Access:** Password-protected.
*   **Content:** Provenance documents, installation guides, past works.

### 6. Contact & Salon
*   **Content:** Single email, phone for appointments, salon address.
*   **Tone:** Privacy and consultation.

## âœ¨ Critical Interaction & Design Features

### Navigation
*   **Style:** Fixed, ultra-thin white line.
*   **Interaction:** Words appear on hover.
*   **Mode:** "Viewing Room" toggle for distraction-free full-screen.

### Typography
*   **Font:** Single premium serif (e.g., Cormorant Garamond).
*   **Usage:** Scale weight/size for hierarchy. Wide letter-spacing.

### Motion & Physics
*   **Scroll:** Weighted parallax. Image moves slower than text. Slow and deliberate.
*   **Physics:** "Magnetic" feel.
*   **Sound:** Optional ambient soundscape (studio sounds).

### Interaction Cues
*   **Prompts:** Use text ("Explore the Form", "Discover the Detail") instead of buttons.
